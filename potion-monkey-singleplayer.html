Potion Monkey

World 1 · Singleplayer

You are a wizard. Battle through the Treetop against a rival monkey wizard. Brew potions, cast spells, and survive all 3 levels.

Singleplayer · vs AI · World 1

📜 How to Play
The Loop

Each round: Shop → Brew → Spellbook → Battle
Win a battle: +10🍌   Lose: +5🍌
Complete 3 levels to finish World 1 and pick up a new monkey.

Potion Types

✨ Spells (Perma)

Passive buffs assigned to monkeys. Always active. No flask needed. Up to 3 per monkey. Order matters for Spirit Water / Boost Berry interactions.

🧪 Consumables

Battle potions that affect your monkey. Flask is returned after use. 2×1 in inventory.

💦 Splashes

Thrown onto the battlefield. Flask is consumed. 2×1 in inventory.

Stats

❤️ HP — health pool
⚔️ ATK — damage per hit
🛡 DEF — flat damage reduction. Damage = max(1, ATK - DEF)
💨 SPD — higher speed attacks first. Ties = simultaneous
